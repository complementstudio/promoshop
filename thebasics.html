The Basics

What:

PromoShop is a privately held, minority owned, full service promotional advertising and marketing services firm, headquartered in Los Angeles, California.

When:

PromoShop was founded in 1998, in order to provide a broader range and higher level of marketing services to our clients. By bringing together a team of creative professionals, the company has thrived, as has its services.

Who:

A rare combination of a large 100% certified minority owned and operated corporation.
