Compliance

At PromoShop, we have a proven track record of producing safe and compliant products. Our suppliers are pre-qualified based on meeting stringent safety and social compliance guidelines

We work with our clients on developing a Quality Assurance Plan based on the product, promotion, method of distribution, age of recipients and, promotion start date. From this information we determine what tests are required and how often tests/quality checks are performed.

We use accredited 3rd party companies to audit the following:

  • Product safety- Policies and procedures with the ability to detect non-compliant product before it ships.
  • Social accountability- Supply chain meeting legal and ethical standards of the UN Global Compact.
  • Environmental stewardship- Promote recycling, water use and resource management.
  • Quality- Quality manual and monitoring, inspections and a supplier qualification program.
  • Supply chain security- Meet criteria established by US Customs C-TPAT program.
